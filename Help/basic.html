Basic Usage

Minimalism is the name of the game with WebDesktop.

Help

Apps

Click on them and have fun! Note: The logo in the top right will send you back to the app selection screen in workspace one.

Workspaces

Workspaces allow you to open multiple apps at the same time. To toggle between workspaces, click on the time in the navagation bar. To resset a workspace back to the app screen click the red resset button for the correct workspace.

Search

Click the search icon in the navigation bar while connected to the internet to be brought to a DuckDuckGo search box in tab one.

Tabs

Click the wifi icon in the navigation bar to toggle or resset any of the four available tabs you can use.


Note: Workspaces and Tabs are limited by design to four to keep all your programs running smootly and without clutter.
